About

I am a tenure-track Associate Professor at the School of Automation and Intelligent Sensing, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My current research focuses on medical world models, surgical machine vision reasoning, medical generative models, and multimodal medical AI.

Before joining Shanghai Jiao Tong University, I was a Postdoctoral Scholar at Stanford University School of Medicine, working on medical generative methods including automatic non-contrast CT enhancement and counterfactual brain MRI image generation. I received my Ph.D. from the University of Technology Sydney, where I worked on knowledge-enhanced medical multimodal pretraining, medical time-series forecasting, and intelligent transportation systems.

Research Interests

  • Medical multimodal foundation models and medical world models
  • Medical report generation, radiology reasoning, and clinical language-vision learning
  • Medical image generation, enhancement, segmentation, and representation learning
  • Surgical machine vision reasoning and trustworthy medical AI
